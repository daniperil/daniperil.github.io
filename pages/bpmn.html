BPMN and Camunda Example

BPMN (Business Process Model and Notation) is a graphical notation for specifying business processes in a workflow. It uses standardized symbols and shapes to represent different types of activities, decisions, events, and more. BPMN provides a way to visualize and communicate complex business processes in a clear and concise way.

Camunda is an open source platform for workflow and business process management. It supports BPMN as its core modeling language and provides tools for designing, executing, and monitoring workflows. Camunda can be used to automate business processes, integrate systems and services, and manage human tasks and decisions.
